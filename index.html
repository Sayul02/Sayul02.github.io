My Biographie!

AThroughout my life, I have dedicated time to both my studies and my personal development.

Studies:
I began my academic education at Carlos L. Olderbourg elementary school, continuing later for two years at Enrique Corona Morfín high school and another two years at Manuel Álvarez high school. I completed my high school studies at the Preparatory School Number 30 of the University of Colima.
Currently, with the goal of continuing to progress, I am in the process of completing my degree in Internet Technology Engineering at the University of Colima.

Professional development:
Since my high school years, I began to accumulate work experience. My first jobs were at a fruit store, then an electronics store. Upon entering high school, I had the opportunity to work in a variety of roles, including floor installer helper, independent delivery man and waiter in a restaurant.
Later, during my undergraduate vacations, I lived for a few months in Mexico City, where I worked in a donut and milkshake shop. Upon returning to my place of residence, I continued my career as a blacksmith and, later, as a waiter at events, an experience I greatly enjoyed. This work allowed me to build relationships, which led to additional opportunities as an event decorator and DJ staff.

Social:
I was a student representative in both junior high and high school. Although I intended to run for the faculty, unfortunately the other slate won the elections. However, this does not stop me and I continue to plan activities. I consider myself a socially active person, with a wide network of contacts who I frequent on a regular basis, as I enjoy socializing very much.
